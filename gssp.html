Goalies Selection Rules

validated:  08 June 2018
1) To be fair on the play time, everyone takes turn playing goalie position and they are pre-assigned before the games.
2) Each Friday game has 4 quarters and there is a change in goalie every quarter. ie. Four goalies per team will be pre-assigned.
3) Admins already keep track of the goalie count since the beginning of this season and we will start from the bottom of player list sorted by ascending first name.
4) This will take effective on this Friday's game (Jun 8).

1) 为了在比赛中公平起见，每个人都轮流打守门员的位置，他们在比赛前预先分配。
2) 每个周五的比赛都有4节，每节守门员都有变化。例如，每队有四名守门员将被预先分配。
3) 自从本赛季开始以来，管理员已经跟踪守门员数量，我们将从球员名单的底部开始按升序排名。
4) 这将在本周五的比赛中（6月8日）生效。

Substitutes Selection

10 May 2018
If sub is required due to size of the team, pre-game random selection will be used to decide sub.

如果由于球队规模过大而需要进行替换安排，则将使用赛前随机选择来决定替补名单。

Goalies Selection

10 May 2018
Any volunteer from the team that wants to play goalie is acceptable; if no volunteer, pre-game random selection will be used to decide goalie.

任何想要做守门员的志愿者都可以接受; 如果没有志愿者，赛前随机选择本方球员做守门员。
