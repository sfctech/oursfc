2019 UEFA Champions League Pool Rules Summary (规则摘要)

1) Rank the 8 playoff teams according to your estimate of their (descending) order of finish.

2) Rank your winner 1st, 2nd, 3rd and 4th ... etc. Your first-ranked team receives 8x2 points for each win, 8 for a draw in a 90mins game, additional 4 points if win in OT/penalty (not apply in the final game). Second-ranked team 7x2 points for each win and 7 for a draw in a 90mins game, additional 4 points if win in OT/penalty (not apply in the final game). Points for each win ... eight-ranked team - 1x2 point for each win. 1 for draw in a 90mins game, additional 4 points if win in OT/penalty (not apply in the final game). There will be 10 bonus points for correctly ranking the UEFA winner (ranked first). Another 10 bonus points each for correctly ranking Arsenal/Chelsea playing in UEFA Europa League. $5 per entry, maximum 4 entries.

3) Please pass on to interested parties. Make payment to Albert Li. Etransfer is also accepted (email: lialbert298@gmail.com). Your picks must be in no later than 2:00pm April 9. 1st game starts on April 9th till June 3rd.

4) Prizes:

    If <25 play, 1st wins 100% prize of the pool; last place finisher gets back $5 from the first place prize money.

    If 25/+ play, will create a 2nd prize, 1st wins 70% prize of the pool; 2nd wins 30%; last place finisher gets back $5 from the first place prize money.

    If 35/+ play, there will be a 3rd prize, 1st wins 60% prize of the pool; 2nd wins 30%; 3rd wins 10%; last place finisher gets back $5 from the first place prize money.

    “Ties Split” Rule applies. If 2 players tie for a given prize, they split that prize and the next (lower) prize combined.
